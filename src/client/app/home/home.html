What is NatureNet?

Citizen Science Project with a Crowd-Sourced Design

NatureNet is a citizen science project with a crowd-sourced design. The goal to increase motivation for participation and to identify creative ways in which people can contribute to sustainable bio-diversity at the Aspen Center for Environmental Studies (ACES). NatureNet is funded by the National Science Foundation (IIS-1221513) in a grant to the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, the University of Maryland, and the University of Colorado. The project is in coordination with the naturalists at ACES.
